Social Bee Lab
Evidence in action. Opportunity in reach.

The Social Bee Lab (Social Behavior and Experimental Economics Laboratory) is a behavioral and experimental economics hub based at UNAB. We identify behavioral barriers that limit access to social and economic opportunities and deliver solutions based on evidence through large scale experiments and open data tools. We also provide training programs to equip students, policymakers, and industry leaders with the skills to turn behavioral insights into real world impact.



Projects

Our portfolio of projects ranges from science-based training programs, to awards & scholarships that boost college students’ opportunities, and a discussion-paper series that shares our latest research. You can meet our team here.

Training
Programs

Strategic Goals Course.

Since 2021, originally as part of an alliance with CBID at NYU Abu Dhabi (UAE) and UNAB (Colombia), we have offered four sessions of the Strategic Goals program. This is a self-paced online course that helps learners set clear objectives, overcome common behavioral barriers, and build lasting confidence. In eight bite-sized sessions grounded in cutting-edge behavioral science, participants can learn practical tactics, like commitment devices, habit tracking, and growth-mindset reframing, to turn aspirations into action.

  • Participation: About 2000 students have completed the course
  • Incentives: Completion certificate (2o00 studentes) and more than 5 iPads

Awards &
Scholarships

In alliance with the Luxembourg Institute of Socio-economic research (LISER) and UNAB (Colombia) we have created the Student Excellence Award in 2025. This is a recognition to high performing college students, that goes beyond achievements of academic scores. The award involves active participation from faculty and peers, aiming to give voice to the entire academic community.

  • Participation: About 600 students have applied or have been nominated for this award.
  • Incentives: Certification of recognition, plus 10 awards of 1 million pesos each

Discussion
Papers

The Social Bee Lab Discussion Paper Series (SBL-DP) disseminates high-quality research conducted at the lab to the scientific community and the interested public worldwide before they are later published in academic journals. This discussion paper series was launched in 2025.
